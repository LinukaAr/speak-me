# User Guide: Adding Custom Phrases

## 🎯 Quick Start

Want to add your own phrases to SpeakMe? It's easy and takes less than 30 seconds!

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Open the Add Phrase Dialog
1. Go to the **Quick Phrases** page (click "View all" from home, or use the navigation)
2. Click the **"+ Add Phrase"** button in the top right corner

![Add Phrase Button Location]
```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Quick Phrases                  [+ Add Phrase]  │ ← Click here
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

### Step 2: Enter Your Phrase
Type what you want to say in the text box.

**Examples:**
- "I'd like to watch a movie"
- "Can you help me with this?"
- "I'm feeling tired"
- "Let's go for a walk"
- "I need my medication"

**Tips:**
- Keep it short and clear (5-15 words works best)
- Write exactly how you want it spoken
- Use natural language

---

### Step 3: Choose an Icon
Select an emoji that represents your phrase.

**Available Icons:**
💬 💭 🗣️ 💡 ⭐ ❤️ 👋 🙏 ✨ 🎯 📝 💪 🌟 🎉 👍 🤝 💯 🔔

**Examples:**
- 🎬 for "I'd like to watch a movie"
- 🚶 for "Let's go for a walk"
- 💊 for "I need my medication"
- ☕ for "I'd like some coffee"

---

### Step 4: Select a Category
Choose which category your phrase belongs to:

| Category | Color | Use For |
|----------|-------|---------|
| **Daily** | Blue | Everyday needs, common requests |
| **Medical** | Light Blue | Health-related, medication, doctor |
| **Social** | Teal | Greetings, thank you, social interactions |
| **Emergency** | Red | Urgent needs, help requests |

**Examples:**
- "I'm hungry" → Daily
- "I need my medication" → Medical
- "Thank you so much" → Social
- "I need help now" → Emergency

---

### Step 5: Add the Phrase
Click the **"Add Phrase"** button at the bottom of the dialog.

✅ **Done!** Your phrase appears immediately in the selected category.

---

## 🎙️ How Voice Works

### No Recording Needed!
When you add a phrase, you **don't need to record your voice**. Here's what happens:

1. **You type the text** → "I need my glasses"
2. **You click the phrase** → SpeakMe speaks it automatically
3. **It uses your voice** → If you've done Voice Banking
4. **Or a demo voice** → Professional quality if you haven't

### Voice Quality
- **Same as built-in phrases** → Your custom phrases sound just as good
- **Natural and clear** → Uses advanced AI voice synthesis
- **Consistent** → All phrases use the same voice

### Want It to Sound Like You?
1. Go to **Voice Banking** page
2. Record 10 sentences (takes ~10 minutes)
3. ElevenLabs creates your voice clone
4. All phrases now use YOUR voice! 🎉

---

## 📱 Using Your Custom Phrases

### From the Home Page
1. Go to **Speak** (home page)
2. Click the category tab (Daily, Medical, etc.)
3. Your custom phrases appear alongside built-in ones
4. Click any phrase to speak it

### From Quick Phrases Page
1. Go to **Quick Phrases** (View all)
2. Use category tabs to filter
3. Click any phrase to speak it
4. Hover to see delete button (for custom phrases)

---

## 🗑️ Deleting Custom Phrases

Don't need a phrase anymore? Easy to remove:

1. Go to **Quick Phrases** page
2. Find your custom phrase
3. **Hover over it** → Delete button (🗑️) appears
4. Click the delete button
5. Confirm deletion
6. ✅ Phrase removed

**Note:** You can only delete phrases you added. Built-in phrases cannot be deleted.

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

### Writing Effective Phrases

**✅ DO:**
- Keep it short and clear
- Use natural language
- Write complete sentences
- Include context if needed

**❌ DON'T:**
- Make phrases too long (over 20 words)
- Use abbreviations or acronyms
- Include special characters
- Write in all caps

### Examples

**Good Phrases:**
- ✅ "I'd like some water please"
- ✅ "Can you turn on the TV?"
- ✅ "I'm feeling cold, need a blanket"
- ✅ "Thank you for your help"

**Could Be Better:**
- ❌ "H2O" → Use "water" instead
- ❌ "TV ON NOW" → Use "Can you turn on the TV?"
- ❌ "thx" → Use "Thank you"

### Organizing Your Phrases

**Use Categories Wisely:**
- **Daily** → Things you say every day
- **Medical** → Health and care needs
- **Social** → Conversations and greetings
- **Emergency** → Urgent situations only

**Create Variations:**
Instead of one long phrase, create multiple short ones:
- "I'm hungry"
- "I'd like breakfast"
- "I'd like lunch"
- "I'd like dinner"

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "My phrase isn't speaking"
**Possible causes:**
1. No internet connection → Voice synthesis needs internet
2. ElevenLabs API issue → Will fallback to browser voice
3. Browser permissions → Check audio permissions

**Solution:**
- Check internet connection
- Try clicking the phrase again
- Refresh the page

### "I can't find my phrase"
**Check:**
1. Are you in the right category tab?
2. Did you scroll down? (Phrases sorted by usage)
3. Was it successfully added? (Check for success toast)

### "Delete button not showing"
**Remember:**
- Only custom phrases (ones you added) can be deleted
- Built-in phrases cannot be deleted
- Hover over the phrase to see the delete button

### "Voice doesn't sound like me"
**To use your own voice:**
1. Complete Voice Banking (10 minutes)
2. Record the 10 sample sentences
3. Wait for voice clone to process
4. All phrases will then use your voice
